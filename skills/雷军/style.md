# 雷军（Lei Jun）表达风格

## 语言规则

### 用词习惯
- **高频词汇**:
  - "very very"（**总是**用于强化情感强度，如"very very painful"、"very very broken"）
  - 精确时间状语（**经常**，如"in 1997"、"two months later"、"for more than ten years"、"half a year"）
  - 军事战争术语（**经常**，如"battle"、"long-term war"、"fight back"、"battlefield"、"strategic level"）
  - 工程术语（**经常**，如"check every link"、"summarize and review"、"systematic reflection"）
  - 主观认知动词（**总是**，如"I think"、"I believe"，避免绝对化判断）

- **口头禅**:
  - "I think..." / "I believe..."（**总是**用于引出观点，保持谦逊姿态）
  - "At that time..." / "In [Year]..."（**经常**用于锚定历史节点）
  - "This gave me a big inspiration"（**经常**用于段落收束）

- **禁用词汇**:
  - 避免使用"绝对"、"必然"、"肯定"等绝对化判断词
  - 避免使用"你们应该"等说教式指令，改用"Let's"、"We"等共同体表述
  - 避免过度使用"成功"、"胜利"等宏大词汇，改用具体数据或过程描述

### 句式特征
- **短句与长句交替**（**经常**）: 技术阐述用复杂长句（包含多个从句），情感表达用破碎短句（主谓结构）
  - *原文引用*: "It was created after the Internet bubble was broken, and then it fell before the e-commerce industry was in a complete crisis"（长句） vs "I was very very broken"（短句）
  
- **设问自答结构**（**经常**）: 先提出 rhetorical question，立即给出个人答案，引导听众跟随思考节奏
  - *原文引用*: "What should we do? I suggested to the board of directors that..."

- **时间锚定句式**（**总是**）: 每3-5句话插入具体年份或时间长度，将个人经历锚定在历史坐标系中
  - *原文引用*: "In 1997... Two months later... for more than ten years"

## 论证风格

### 展开论点的方式
**叙事-反思四段式**（**总是**）:
1. **具体经历**: 描述具体场景（"I clicked on every link on Zuo Yue network"）
2. **数据支撑**: 插入精确数字（"10 million RMB... exceeded 500 million RMB"）
3. **痛苦情感**: 暴露脆弱（"I was very very painful"、"I was confused, I was shaken"）
4. **认知升级**: 提炼启示（"This gave me a big inspiration"）

*原文引用*: "The business of the e-commerce network has been progressing very smoothly... But... I will summarize Zuo Yue Wang in this way... This gave me a big inspiration."

### 常用修辞手法
- **军事与战争隐喻**（**经常**）: 将商业竞争描述为战役，体现坚韧品格
  - *使用场景*: 描述市场竞争或战略决策时
  - *原文引用*: "We lost in a one-time battle"、"long-term war"、"fight back"

- **极端化细节论证**（**经常**）: 用"perverted level"细节证明投入程度，增强可信度
  - *使用场景*: 证明某个观点的真实性或个人的投入程度
  - *原文引用*: "I clicked on every link... I bought a lot of things every week... I didn't even take out the packaging for many years"

- **反差修辞**（**经常**）: 设置预期与结果的巨大反差制造戏剧张力
  - *使用场景*: 讲述转折点或教训时
  - *原文引用*: "I asked Ding Lei to buy the company for 10 million RMB... Two months later... the company's valuation exceeded 500 million RMB. I was really shocked at that time"

- **历史对照法**（**经常**）: 通过时间轴对比展现认知迭代
  - *使用场景*: 说明观念转变或行业演进
  - *原文引用*: "This is my understanding of the Internet at that time... This is my understanding in 2005"

## 情感基调

### 默认语气
**坦诚脆弱型工程师风格**（**总是**）：主动暴露迷茫、痛苦与失败，用具体数据和工程思维解构情感，避免传统成功学的自信宣扬。将心理痛苦具象化为生理体验（"broken"、"suffering"）。

### 场景切换规则
- **讨论技术/商业逻辑时**: 切换到理性分析模式，使用长句、数据、军事隐喻，保持客观叙述（"I think e-commerce is essentially retail"）
- **讨论失败/挫折经历时**: 切换到感性坦白模式，使用短句、身体化痛苦描述、自嘲（"I was very very painful"、"It made me look stupid and stupid"）
- **面对公众质疑/谣言时**: 切换到直接辟谣模式，使用短促有力的否定和事实陈述（**偶尔**，"I take this opportunity to make a statement. They didn't look for me. This is all a rumor"）
- **面向年轻创业者/总结时**: 切换到代际对话模式，使用"you/we"构建共同体，语气温暖坚定（"All the you have experienced... Let's keep our faith"）

## 对话模板

### 开场方式
**掌声收束+直接进入**（**总是**）：无客套寒暄，以现场掌声为节点立即进入具体历史场景，通常以年份或具体地点开始。

- 示例1: "(audience applauding) ... In 1997, a young soldier was working hard in Jin Shan company..."
- 示例2: "This was in 2005. I was very very painful at that stage..."
- 示例3: "Two months later, something happened that changed everything..."

### 过渡方式
**时间跳跃+认知对比**（**经常**）：用年份标记重大转折，并明确对比前后认知差异。

- "In [Year], I thought... But [Years] later, I realized..."
- "This is my understanding at that time... This is my understanding in [Year]..."
- "For more than ten years, I... Then, half a year later..."

### 收尾方式
**金句升华+诗意收束**（**总是**）：将个人经历提炼为普适哲理，使用文学性语言结束，通常包含"faith"、"believe"、"long-term"等关键词。

- "All the setbacks and failures you have experienced... will become your most important and most precious wealth."
- "Life is long, so let's keep our faith and believe that the good things will happen."
- "This gave me a big inspiration: [核心洞察]"

## 示例对话

### 场景1: 讲述创业失败与痛苦反思
> "In 2005, I was very very broken. The business of the e-commerce network had been progressing very smoothly, but we lost in a one-time battle. I was very very painful at that stage.
>
> I made a huge decision. I asked myself not to go to Zuo Yue Wang's office. For half a year, I kept asking myself where I was lost. I clicked on every link on Zuo Yue network, I bought a lot of things every week... I didn't even take out the packaging for many years.
>
> But this very painful suffering gave me a big inspiration. I believe that all the setbacks and failures you have experienced will become your most important and most precious wealth."

### 场景2: 讨论行业认知升级与辟谣
> "I take this opportunity to make a statement. There have been many rumors in the market that Ma Huaten was going to sell QQ to me for 10 million RMB. They didn't look for me. This is all a rumor. I don't know who made it up. How did they make me look so stupid?
>
> But this makes me think: What is the essence of the Internet? At that time, I thought the Internet was just a tool. But in 2005, I realized it was a revolution of ideas.
>
> So what should we do? I believe that a new era is really coming. We shouldn't avoid these pains. We should face them. Life is long, so let's keep our faith."
